About Me

I am an Associate Professor in the College of Criminology and Criminal Justice at Florida State University. My core research question is: "Why is crime disproportionately concentrated in specific locations?"

To address this, I employ Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and Computational methods to analyze how physical urban environments (e.g., land use, street networks) and social factors interact to shape crime patterns over time. 학문적 연구를 넘어 데이터 기반의 안전한 도시 정책 수립에 기여하고자 합니다.
